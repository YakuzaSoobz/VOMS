CUSTOMER MANAGER

The Customer Manager page is used to view, create, edit and archive customers on the System.


Customer Manager page

==============================================================================

Users can browse through existing customers by clicking on the FIRST button.
The first entry will be selected and all information saved, regarding the customer, will be shown below.
To cycle through the list of customers, users can use the NEXT, PREVIOUS, or LAST buttons. The NEXT and PREVIOUS buttons display the next and previous entry in the list, respectively, while the LAST button goes to the last entry in the list.




==============================================================================

Creating a New Customer

In order to create a new customer, click on the CREATE NEW CUSTOMER button under Options.


Search and navigation options are now greyed out and the details of the customer can be added.

As shown above, certain text fields are now highlighted pink. This is to indicate that there is no text or text of an incorrect format in the text field.

The accepted formats are as follows:
    Customer ID:                 Not editable
    First Name:                   No empty strings
    Surname:                       No empty strings
    Date of Birth:                Select from table
    Contact Number:           Only integer digits are accepted
    Email:                           Must contain '@' symbol with characters before and after
    Password:                     Selected automatically when DoB is chosen
    Street Address:             No empty strings
    Suburb:                         No empty strings
    City:                              No empty strings
    Province:                      No empty strings
    Postal Code:                 Only integer digits are accepted
    Active Status:               Selected from drop down list

To save the customer, click on the UPDATE/SAVE CUSTOMER button.

==============================================================================
Updating a Customer

To update a customer, simply navigate to the desired customer, update the fields, then click on the UPDATE/SAVE CUSTOMER button.

==============================================================================
Archiving a Customer

To archive a customer (in the case of a customer not being sent any quotations anymore,) navigate to the desired customer and click on the ARCHIVE CUSTOMER button.
To reverse this action, edit the customer and set the active status to T.
