WHAT IS THE VOMS?

The Vendor Order Management System is a bespoke software system developed by Normalisers42 to service Mhlathuze Industrial Suppliers and Services cc. It serves as a medium through which employees and managers can create, view, update and archive a multitude of entries that are valuable to the function and growth of the company.
Throughout the Vendor Order Management System an array of Tooltips are laid out to make navigation simple and intuitive. Simply hover over a button or text box if you're unsure about how to navigate or what text to enter. For more information, you may click on the 'Help' button at any time and it will display information on how each form is meant to be used.

Login Screen:
To access the Vendor Order Management System you are required to enter your personal Employee ID as well as your secret passsword.
Access will be granted to the Vendor Order Management System once your identity is verified depending on the Admin Level selected.

Admin Level 1 is for an Employee. Admin Level 2 is for a Manager.
